Abstract

structural constants unlocks a coherent narrative of dimensional extraction: the breaking of the 144-grid of
the “Four-Cornered Fortress” (145 (mod 144) = 1), the resolution of the Sothic time-loop (1460 ≈10 ×
145), and the harmonic shattering of the “Glass Fortress.” Furthermore, we synthesize these ﬁndings with
Somatic Bio-Geometric Harmonics, positioning the number 145 not merely as a mathematical abstraction
but as an experiential resonance key—an “indexical cipher”—that bridges the gap between the material grid
and the “Diamond Body” of human consciousness.
