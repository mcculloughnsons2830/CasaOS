## Short #2 — "The Poison" (hook: resentment)

**(0–3s)** [close-up portrait footage] VO: "Anthony Hopkins says holding a
grudge is like death."

poison yourself and waiting for the other person to die. The past cannot be
renegotiated. Carry it, and it buries you before your heart stops."

**(20–40s)** VO: "He should know. He spent the 1970s at war with everyone —
because he was at war with himself. The day he put it down is the day his
life started."

**(40–48s)** VO: "What's the grudge you already know you should drop?"

## Short #3 — "Certainty" (hook: contrarian)

**(0–3s)** [historical footage montage] VO: "Anthony Hopkins says the most
dangerous people in history all had one thing in common."

world down."

that we know nothing. Wear that lightly, and you become impossible to
insult — and very hard to fool."
