# Full Marketing Plan — "Before the Curtain"

Working brand name: **Before the Curtain** — late-life wisdom of icons, told as
cinematic video essays. (Verify handle/trademark availability before public
launch; fallback names: *Wisdom of Years*, *The Final Act*, *Elder Light*.)

This plan operationalizes `../youtube-channel-strategy.md` into a concrete
brand, launch sequence, and 90-day execution calendar.

---

## 1. Brand Identity

**Promise:** "The things legends only understand at the end — delivered while
you still have time to use them."

**Voice:** calm, reverent, unhurried. Never hype, never clickbait-cynical.
Reads like a eulogy that turned out to be a love letter to being alive.

**Visual system:**
- Palette: warm charcoal (#1C1A17), aged gold (#C9A45C), parchment (#EFE6D5),
  deep burgundy accent (#6B2C2C)
- Typography: serif display for titles (Playfair Display / Freight), humanist
  sans for UI/captions (Inter)
- Imagery: close-up portraiture, chiaroscuro lighting, film grain, warm
  desaturated grade
- Logo: a stage curtain reduced to a minimal gold arc/drape mark over charcoal
- Thumbnail template: left-weighted portrait, right-weighted 3–5 word serif
  quote in parchment, thin gold rule underneath — identical layout every video

**Tagline options:** "Life is not a rehearsal." (borrowed sentiment — use as
theme, not trademark) / "Wisdom, before the curtain falls." / "What they know
at the end."

## 2. Audience & Positioning

- **Primary:** 35–65+, majority male skew typical of the niche, watching on TV
  and desktop in the evening. High watch-time, high ad RPM demographic.
- **Psychographic:** quietly anxious about time, career plateaus, aging
  parents, own mortality; allergic to hustle-culture motivation.
- **Positioning vs. competitors:** generic motivation channels sell energy; we
  sell *perspective*. Every asset should feel like it was made by hand, slowly.

## 3. Channel Funnel Architecture

```
Shorts (discovery) → Flagship essays (depth/watch-time) → Community posts +
comments (habit) → Newsletter (owned audience) → future owned-IP interviews
```

- **Shorts** = top of funnel. Each flagship yields 3–5 Shorts pinned back to it.
- **Flagship essays** = the product. One icon + one thesis, 12–20 min.
- **Newsletter** ("One Thing Before the Curtain," weekly, one distilled lesson
  + link to the video) = the asset we own if the algorithm turns.

## 4. Launch Plan (Weeks 1–2)

**Week 1 — build in private:**
1. Secure @BeforeTheCurtain (or fallback) on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X;
   register beforethecurtain.com or .co; set up newsletter list (Beehiiv/
   ConvertKit free tier).
2. Apply brand kit: avatar, banner, thumbnail template, end card, quote-card
   template (assets in `assets/`).
3. Finish pilot: Hopkins essay (`pilot-script.md`) — record VO, edit, grade.
4. Cut 3 Shorts from the pilot (`shorts-scripts.md`), captioned, vertical.
5. Load channel: about-page copy, channel trailer = 45s cut of the pilot's
   cold open, playlists pre-created ("Actors," "Artists," "Thinkers").

**Week 2 — launch sequence:**
- Day 1: publish pilot flagship at 5pm local (evening TV viewing window).
- Day 1–3: one Short per day, each pinned-comment linking the flagship.
- Day 2: first community post — quote card + "Whose wisdom should we tell
  next?" poll (this poll doubles as content research forever).
- Day 4: publish newsletter #1 (`launch-copy.md`) to friends/family seed list.
- Day 7: publish flagship #2 (Eastwood). Two videos in week one signals
  cadence to both the algorithm and early subscribers.

## 6. Discovery & SEO

- **Search targets (title/description/tags):** "[icon name] life advice",
  "[icon name] speech", "[icon name] motivational", "wisdom of [icon]",
  "[icon name] on life", plus evergreen: "life advice from old people",
  "advice for your 30s/40s/50s".
- **Title pattern:** `[Name] at [age]: [emotional promise]` — e.g. "Anthony
  Hopkins at 87: The Wake-Up Call That Saved His Life."
- **Description template:** 2-sentence hook → chapter timestamps (retention +
  search) → sources list (credibility + defamation hygiene) → newsletter link.
- **Chapters named as quotes**, not topics ("'My struggle was over'" beats
  "The turning point") — they get read in search results.

## 7. Distribution Beyond YouTube

- **Instagram Reels / TikTok / YouTube Shorts:** same vertical cuts, native
  captions, no watermarks crossing platforms. Bio links to newsletter, not
  the channel (own the audience).
- **X/Twitter:** quote-card threads — 5 lines from the essay, video linked in
  the last post.
- **Reddit:** contribute, don't spam — answer "advice" threads genuinely and
  link only when directly relevant.
- **Podcast pitch (month 2–3):** offer wisdom-niche podcasts a "3 lessons from
  [icon]" guest segment; it's cheap authority-building.

## 8. Community & Retention Loops

- Pin a question, not a CTA, on every video ("What's the grudge you know you
  should drop?") — comment velocity in hour one feeds suggested reach.
- Heart + reply to every comment for the first 90 days, in brand voice.
- Monthly "you chose" video from poll results — makes subscribers co-authors.

## 10. KPIs & Kill Criteria

- **North stars:** 30-second retention ≥ 70% (hook), avg % viewed ≥ 45%
  (story), subs/view ratio ≥ 1.5% (packaging-audience fit).
- **Weekly review:** retention graphs per video; retitle/rethumbnail anything
  under-indexing after 72h.
- **Day-90 checkpoint:** if no video has broken 10k views AND retention
  targets aren't met, the *format* is fine but the *packaging or icon selection*
  isn't — pivot icon selection (poll data tells you where), don't pivot format.
- **Monetization milestone:** 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours → apply to YPP
  immediately.

## 11. Legal/Ethical Marketing Rules (restated, they apply to marketing too)

- No implication of endorsement by the icons ("fan-made tribute essay" framing
  in about page and descriptions).
- Editorial-use-safe or licensed portraits only in thumbnails and social art —
  or commissioned illustration (safest; also more brandable).
- No AI voice clones of real people, anywhere, including ads and Shorts.
- Keep the per-asset license log current (`assets/LICENSES.md`).
