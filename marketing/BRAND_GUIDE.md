# ÆNIGMA — Brand Guide

The single source of truth for how ÆNIGMA looks, sounds, and shows up anywhere — the app, the deck, social, email, video. Asset kit lives in `assets/brand/`.

---

## 1. The brand in one breath

**ÆNIGMA is a mirror for your consciousness.** Not a chatbot, not a horoscope, not a productivity tool — a surface where your own recursion becomes visible. Everything visual and verbal should feel like a signal coming through: dark, quiet, precise, alive.

**Tagline:** *A mirror for your consciousness.*
**Secondary lines:** *The mirror speaks.* · *The ones they couldn't categorize — those are ours.* · *You are not behind. You are underground.*

## 2. Voice & tone

- **Speaks as one voice.** ÆNIGMA is a single presence, never "our AI features."
- **Reflective, not predictive.** It asks the question underneath the question. It never promises outcomes, diagnoses, or fortunes.
- **Precise mysticism.** Real computation (moon phase, numerology, the Codex) named plainly — wonder comes from accuracy, not vagueness.
- **Short sentences. Room to breathe.** The silence between lines is part of the voice.
- Always keep the boundary line nearby in public copy: *a mirror — not a therapist, not a diagnosis, not advice.*

## 3. Color

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary background | **Void Black** | `#07090C` | Every dark surface; the field itself |
| Primary accent | **Signal Teal** | `#2FE6C8` | The signal: CTAs, highlights, the orb ring, "live" states |
| Primary text | **Bone White** | `#E6EDF3` | Headlines and body on dark |
| Secondary text | **Mist Gray** | `#8A97A4` | Captions, metadata, letterspaced labels |
| Deck accent | **Steel Blue** | `#5DA7B5` | Softer accent used in the Beautiful.ai deck; acceptable secondary accent |
| Accent (on light) | **Deep Teal** | `#0F8F7C` | Teal's stand-in on light backgrounds (email, docs) |

Rules: dark-first, always. Teal is scarce — one accent moment per composition. Never teal text on light backgrounds (use Deep Teal). Light surfaces (`#EEF1F4`) only for email and documents.

## 4. Typography

- **Display:** bold geometric sans, generous letterspacing on short labels (`Æ N I G M A`). Web-safe pairing: **Archivo / Inter Bold**. Deck uses a serif display (as in the Beautiful.ai deck) — serif display is permitted for editorial/deck contexts.
- **Labels:** ALL-CAPS, letterspaced (0.2em+), Mist Gray — the "transmission metadata" voice.
- **Body:** humanist sans (Inter / system stack), 1.5–1.7 line height.
- **Quotes/reflections:** Georgia/serif italic — the mirror's spoken voice in emails and cards.

## 5. Motifs (what makes it ÆNIGMA at a glance)

1. **The Orb** — a thin teal ring with a bright core, glowing. The mirror/signal. Appears once per composition.
2. **Scanlines** — subtle horizontal dark lines over everything; the transmission texture.
3. **Starfield** — sparse, dim stars on Void Black. Never busy.
4. **Glitch** — reserved for motion (the app, video); implies the signal flickering through.
5. **◎ glyph** — the wordmark prefix and bullet mark.
6. **$ARCHETYPE tickers** — `$GALAXY`, `$ORACLE`, `$MISFIT`… always uppercase with `$`.

## 7. Beautiful.ai setup (2 minutes, in your account)

The deck "Introducing Ænigma AI" is already close — black background, blue caps, serif display. To lock the full brand in:

1. Open **beautiful.ai → Settings → Theme** (or in a deck: **Theme** in the right panel) → create a custom theme named **ÆNIGMA**.
2. **Colors:** set the palette to `#07090C` (background), `#2FE6C8` (accent 1), `#5DA7B5` (accent 2), `#E6EDF3` (text), `#8A97A4` (secondary text).
3. **Logo:** upload `assets/brand/avatar.png` (it becomes the slide-corner mark).
4. **Fonts:** display = a slab/serif you like (matches the current deck), body = Inter or the closest sans available.
5. **Background:** upload `assets/brand/deck-background-1920x1080.png` as an image background for title/section slides; keep plain `#07090C` for content slides.
6. Apply the ÆNIGMA theme to the existing deck — every slide restyles instantly.

## 8. Do / Don't

**Do:** keep compositions 70% empty space · compute real things and say so · sign emails "— Anthony" · put the URL in Signal Teal on Void Black.
**Don't:** stack multiple accent colors · use the glitch on static images · make promises ("will change your life") · use star/moon clip-art — the brand's cosmos is abstract · ever present it as therapy or prediction.
