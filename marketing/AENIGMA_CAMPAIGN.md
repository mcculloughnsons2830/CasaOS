# ÆNIGMA Beta — Launch Campaign (14-Day Drip)

Goal: get the free beta (https://casaos-production.up.railway.app) in front of
your audience, convert followers into testers, and collect real feedback —
without burning out your feed. The engine is the **10 archetype cards** in
`assets/campaign/` (one shareable graphic per archetype) plus the launch and
follow-up copy below.

**Golden rules for the whole run**
- Post to Facebook + Instagram every day of the drip; mirror the best ones to X and (sparingly) to relevant Groups.
- Every post ends with the link OR "link in bio." Never a post without a path to the app.
- Reply to every comment in the first hour — comment velocity is the single biggest reach lever.
- Ask a question in each post. "Which is loudest in you?" turns a scroll into a comment.
- Pin the Day 1 launch post to the top of your profile for the full two weeks.
