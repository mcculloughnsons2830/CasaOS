## Store SEO & operations

- Product titles: `[Product name] — [keyword phrase]` under 70 chars; keywords from
  `SHORTS_PACK.md` §4 ("urban misfit streetwear", "glow hoodie", "blueprint tee").
- Every description: one story line + one product-spec line + one identity line.
  No hype promises (bible §5).
- Product photos: 4:5 for store, 9:16 crops feed straight into merch-tease Shorts.
- BigCartel for storefront; BuyMeACoffee tiers for membership (uncensored
  transmissions, early drops, high-res art). Cross-link both in every bio.
- Fulfillment note: launch print-on-demand for tees/posters to validate before
  committing to embroidered hoodie inventory; the hoodie's embroidery is the
  premium differentiator, so sample it before listing.
