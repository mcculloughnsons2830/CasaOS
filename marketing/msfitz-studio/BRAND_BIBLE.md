# Ms'FitZ Studio — Brand Bible

The single source of truth for the Studio's content brand: the glowing-eyed misfit
mascots, the rain-soaked streets, the awakening arc. Everything the Studio publishes
should be recognizable from three frames with the sound off.

**Core identity:** *Misfits who see the Signal.*

---

## 1. Values & positioning

- **Authenticity** — raw over polished. The rain stays in the shot.
- **Sovereignty** — nobody hands you permission. "Stay sovereign."
- **Awakening** — the arc of every piece: exhaustion → signal → glow.
- **Responsibility** — the Stan Lee ethos: power obligates. Rebellion without responsibility is just noise.
- **Community among outsiders** — the isolation is temporary; the Signal finds its own.

**Positioning:** the intersection of streetwear culture, alternative-history storytelling,
and personal development — delivered through lovable but edgy anthropomorphic misfits.
Cinematic urban grit meets mystical awakening.

## 2. Naming conventions (use exactly)

| Name | Refers to | Where it appears |
|---|---|---|
| **Ms'FitZ Studio** | The creative house (legal/brand entity) | Credits, About pages, footers — matches `../BRAND_GUIDE.md` |
| **Ms'FitZ Society** | The community/voice | Newsletters, community posts |
| **MisFitZ Outcast** | The apparel/mascot wordmark (the rabbit's hoodie text) | Merch, on-character graphics only |
| **@MsfitZ-Vibe** | The channel watermark | Video watermarks, end-cards |

Rule: the Studio and Society keep the apostrophe (`Ms'FitZ`); the apparel wordmark
does not (`MisFitZ`). Never mix them on the same surface without checking this table.

## 3. Mascot family

| Character | Role | Look | Energy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Rabbit** (primary) | Contemplative, rebellious everyman — the audience's stand-in | Green "MisFitZ Outcast" hoodie, gold chain, luminous **orange** eyes, oversized sneakers | Quiet intensity; sits in the rain, headphones on; the one who stayed awake |
| **The Moth** (secondary) | The chaotic, nocturnal voice of the outcast | Denim jacket with anarchy patch, **pink/magenta** eyes, "Loser" neon signage nearby | Night-rebel; erratic, magnetic, drawn to the light everyone else avoids |
| **The Bear** | The Studio's mark — premium/logo contexts | Hooded bear, amber eyes, clean white/black lockup (see `../assets/brand/msfitz-studio-logo.png`) | Institutional; never memes |
| **The Owl** | Mystical elder — long-form narration seasons | Earlier mystical design | Ancient-knowledge drops |
| **Hulk-Rabbit** | Power-fantasy variant | Oversized, post-awakening rabbit | Sparingly — payoff moments only |
| **Blue blocky character** | Light explainers | Simple, friendly geometry | Tutorials, store how-tos, disclaimers |

**Duality rule:** the Rabbit and the Moth are the brand's two poles — contemplative
rebel vs. chaotic voice. Most content features one; "conversation" pieces may use both.
The Bear never appears *in scenes* — it is the mark, not a character.

**Consistent traits (non-negotiable):** glowing eyes (orange/teal/pink), oversized
hoodies and sneakers, wet urban streets, graffiti backdrops, rain/fog, and sacred
geometry or energy-particle effects reserved for "awakening" beats.

## 4. Color

Core palette is shared with ÆNIGMA (`../BRAND_GUIDE.md` §3); the Studio adds its own accents.

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Background | **Void Black** | `#07090C` | Every surface; deep charcoal `#14171C` for mid-ground buildings/fog |
| Signal accent | **Signal Teal** | `#2FE6C8` | The Signal itself: energy particles, one accent moment per composition |
| Text | **Bone White** | `#E6EDF3` | Overlay text, captions |
| Eye glow / energy | **Luminous Orange** | `#FF8C1A` | The Rabbit's eyes; awakening energy; gold chain highlights (`#E8B44A` for gold) |
| Night-rebel accent | **Neon Pink** | `#FF3D8E` | The Moth's eyes; neon signage; night scenes only |
| Giza accent | **Desert Sand** | `#D9B382` | Giza Protocol content; pairs with **Burnt Orange** `#C4571F` |
| Graffiti pops | — | any, small doses | Background walls only; never on the character or text |

Rules: dark-first, always. One glow color dominates per piece (orange for Rabbit
pieces, pink for Moth pieces, teal for Signal/ÆNIGMA crossovers). Compositions stay
~70% dark space — the glow reads because the frame is empty.

## 5. Voice & tone

**Poetic grit** — raw street wisdom meets ancient insight. Short and punchy for
social; layered for long-form.

- Short declaratives. Room to breathe between lines.
- Second person, direct. "You" before "we"; "we" only for the community beat.
- Ancient-knowledge drops land as *questions and metaphors*, never as claims of fact.
  The Giza material is framed as "what if" storytelling and inner-power metaphor.
- One emoji max per caption (🔥 is the house emoji). No hashtag walls — 3 targeted tags.
- Never: hype-guru promises ("this will change your life"), victim framing, punching down.

**Calibration line (the vibe test):**
> "Sitting in the rain, headphones on, eyes glowing. They call us misfits.
> We're just the ones who stayed awake."

If a caption couldn't sit next to that line, rewrite it.

## 6. Narrative arcs (every piece maps to one)

1. **Exhaustion → Awakening** — the mask-shedding arc. Pressure, static, then the glow.
2. **Outsider isolation → Collective Signal** — alone in the rain, then the community call.
3. **The Pyramid** — Giza as metaphor for inner/outer power: "the reactor inside is waking up."
4. **Power → Responsibility** — the Stan Lee arc; the glow obligates.

## 7. Visual rules

- Glowing eyes appear in **every** piece. The glow pulse is the brand's heartbeat.
- Urban rain/neon in every street scene; fog for contemplative beats.
- Watermark `@MsfitZ-Vibe` bottom-center on all video, matching the existing video frames.
- Awakening beats only: sacred geometry overlays, energy particles, Signal Teal.
- Giza content: desert sand/burnt orange grade, blueprint/diagram overlays, industrial
  pyramid renders — always cut back to a mascot as narrator/guide.
- Merch photography: wet-street or rooftop-neon settings, eye-glow added in post.
- End-cards: Subscribe prompt + watermark; long-form closes with the co-branded
  credit lockup (`../assets/brand/credit-lockup-dark.png`).

## 9. Do / Don't

**Do:** keep 70% of the frame dark · one glow color per piece · end every Short with
the watermark + "Full transmission in bio" · frame Giza as story and metaphor · credit
per `../BRAND_GUIDE.md` on outward surfaces · let silence and rain carry the mood.

**Don't:** stack glow colors · present alternative history as established fact ·
use the Bear as a scene character · promise outcomes · post without the eye glow ·
chase trends that break the rain-noir grade.
